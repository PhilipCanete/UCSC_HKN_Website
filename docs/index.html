A Little About Us

Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) is the national honor society for Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Computer Science at UC Santa Cruz.

Members consist of students, alumni, and other professionals who have demonstrated exceptional academic and professional accomplishments.

At UCSC, HKN provides free tutoring and hosts a variety of workshops and events to promote technical, professional, and academic development.
